Step 1 - Measure

You will receive a tight fitting and check colored garment from Trialroom.me . This garment is our measuring tape.

Step 2 - Mobile app

Click your photograph with our secure mobile application, in this garment, to get your measurements.

Step 3 - No identity

Our application extracts your imporant body features from the picture and sends to us a list of numbers instead, like measurements of a tailor.

Step 4 - Trial Room

Browse garments on websites affiliated with TrailRoom.me and see how garments look on you.

Step 5 - Virtual Closet

Your virtual closet is ready. You can start adding to it, the items that look good on you.
