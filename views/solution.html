Step 1 - Measure

You will receive a tight fitting garment (with geometrical design patterns) from trialroom.me . This garment is our measuring tape.

Step 2 - Mobile app

Wearing this garment, click a photograph of yourself from our secure mobile application. This gives us your measurements.

Step 3 - No identity

Our application extracts your imporant body features from the picture and sends to us a list of numbers instead, like measurements of a tailor.

Step 4 - Trial room

Browse garments on websites affiliated with trailRoom.me and see how garments look on you.

Step 5 - Virtual closet

Your virtual closet is ready. You can start adding clothes from online stores to it.
